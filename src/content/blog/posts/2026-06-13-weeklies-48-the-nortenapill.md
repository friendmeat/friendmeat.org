## __what i'm watching__
### _Les Barbouze_ (1964)
A firmly middle-of-the-road French comedy. Lots of stuff that goes over my head cuz I'm not familiar with mid-century French politics.

Unfortunately weirdly racist towards the Asian cast. There's a dozen-plus Japanese grunts and they don't even get ex-post-facto credits on IMDB. And in all their scenes, the soundtrack is replaced with a morse tone, as if they're drones being controlled via radio. Which reminds me of the racist dehumanization of Chinese communists as "brainwashed" as discussed in the season of the Blowback podcast about [the Korean war](https://blowback.show/Season-3).

Modern films don't have the bravery to cut the soundtrack awkwardly in the middle of a scene. Get those fuckers back in the cutting room with scissors and glue.
