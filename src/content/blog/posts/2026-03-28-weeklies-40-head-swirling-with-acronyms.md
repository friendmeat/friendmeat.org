## __something i hated__
### hard drives? in this economy? part 2: the SATA-SAS debacle
After last week's failure I adjusted my expectations. My head was swirling with acronyms-- CBR, OEM, SAS, SATA, PKI, RAID 0... I finally settled on a couple refurbished 8TB hard drives. When I tried to install them to the [Synology DiskStation DS223j](https://www.newegg.com/synology-ds223j-2-bay-realtek-rtd1619b-processor-diskless-system/p/N82E16822108836) only one would fit in the slot. Turns out I bought two hard drives with SAS-type connections instead of SATA-type. And despite the Synology accepting one of the SAS connections, the board doesn't support the SAS protocol. 

So my stupid ass has to mail back $500 of hard drives on Monday and try again.
